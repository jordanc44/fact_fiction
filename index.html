Discover unknown truths.


At Fact/Fiction, we hope to set you on the path of self-discovery.

Maybe you discover a new passion? Or a new cause?

Maybe you learn that you're really not that interested in long movies with little to no action but lots of dialogue?

Maybe you gain empathy for those across the world?

Maybe you laugh? Maybe you cry?

Or maybe you discover it's just not possible to stay awake past 8pm.

Film List:

All films start at 8pm CT. Discussion to follow on group facetime.

Kiss the Ground

Where to watch: Netflix

A revolutionary group of activists, scientists, farmers, and politicians band together in a global movement of "Regenerative Agriculture" that could balance our climate, replenish our vast water supplies, and feed the world.

Wrinkles the Clown

Where to watch: Hulu

Mysterious videos appear on the Internet of a dangerous clown who likes to scare misbehaving children in Florida.

LFG

Where to watch: HBO Max

It is a no-holds-barred, inside account of the U.S. Women's National Soccer Team's ongoing fight for equal pay.

The Rescue

Where to watch: Disney+

A chronicle of the enthralling, against-all-odds story that transfixed the world in 2018: the daring rescue of twelve boys and their coach from deep inside a flooded cave in Northern Thailand.

Becoming

Where to watch: Netflix

Former first lady Michelle Obama takes an intimate look at her life, hopes, and connections with others.

Too Funny to Fail

Where to watch: Hulu

Creators, cast and network executives recount the strange history of the Dana Carvey Show, the irreverent prime time sketch comedy show that aired for eight episodes on ABC, following its star's meteoric run on Saturday Night Live.
